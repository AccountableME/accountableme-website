Terms & Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

SMS Messaging Consent

By checking the consent box on our signup form, you agree to receive SMS messages from AccountableME, including accountability nudges, goal reminders, and product updates.

Here's what to expect:

  • Message frequency: Up to 12 SMS messages per month (approximately 3 per week), depending on your goal settings and nudge preferences.
  • Message types: Accountability nudges when you haven't checked in on a goal, goal reminders, partner notifications, and occasional product updates.
  • Message and data rates may apply. Contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further SMS messages.

Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchase. You can use AccountableME without SMS -- in-app notifications will still function normally.

What Information We Collect

When you sign up for the AccountableME beta, we collect your email address and, if you opt in to SMS, your phone number (collected during onboarding). If you create an account, we also collect the goals you set, check-in data, and your accountability partner preferences.

We use this information to operate the product, send you the nudges and notifications you've opted into, and improve the service.

How We Use Your Data

  • Account operation: Managing your goals, Integrity Score, and accountability partner connections.
  • Nudges and notifications: Sending in-app, email, and SMS nudges based on your activity and preferences.
  • AI coaching: Your goal and check-in data is processed by AI to personalize the timing and content of nudges. This data is not used to train AI models.
  • Product improvement: Aggregated, anonymized usage data helps us improve the product. We do not sell your personal data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the service providers necessary to operate AccountableME:

  • Twilio: SMS delivery
  • OpenAI: AI-powered nudge personalization
  • AWS: Cloud infrastructure and data storage
  • Stripe / Apple / Google: Payment processing

Your accountability partner can see whether you've checked in on shared goals and your Integrity Score -- that's the product working as designed. They cannot see your private goals or any data you haven't explicitly shared.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes.

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing info@accountableme.net.
